About

Kara Cerf

Styling since 2013

It started on a back porch in Colorado when Kara was 13 — scissors in hand, her siblings in the chair. What began as curiosity grew into something she couldn't walk away from. In 2013, she left school to follow the pull she'd always felt, and by 2015 she had her cosmetology license, a master makeup artist certification, and a career already in motion at an Aveda salon in West Seattle. She didn't stop there. An instructor's license, an esthetics license, and years spent teaching the next generation of stylists followed — including a role as Executive Director of Washington Beauty School. After a decade of building, teaching, and refining her craft across salons and specialty hair replacement work, she opened The Gilded Edge: a space entirely her own.

The Gilded Edge exists because great hair should feel personal. Kara brings over a decade of licensed experience across cosmetology, esthetics, makeup artistry, and education into every appointment — whether you're after a precision cut, a lived-in color, a clean fade, or something you've never quite been able to articulate but always wanted. No fuss. Just skilled hands, real conversation, and work you'll actually love.

Questions

What to know before you book

Do you take walk-ins?

Booking in advance through Vagaro is recommended to secure your preferred time. Same-day availability can be checked directly through the booking link or by calling or texting us.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online anytime through Vagaro using the Book Now button, or reach out by calling or texting (206) 588-6694. We'll get you set up.

Do I need a consultation for color services?

Yes — all color and chemical services require a consultation before booking. Consultations can be done in person or over text for most situations. Reach out and your stylist will be in touch to discuss your goals and formulate a plan.

What areas do you serve?

We are located in the Delridge neighborhood of West Seattle at 5216 Delridge Way SW, Unit A. We welcome clients from across Seattle and the greater Puget Sound area.

Do you cut children's hair?

Yes — children ages 6 and up are welcome. Children's haircuts are priced the same as the equivalent adult service.

Do you offer makeup services?

Yes. Kara is a certified master makeup artist available for weddings, special occasions, photoshoots, Halloween, and theatrical productions. Book a free consultation to get started.
